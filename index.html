Features

Create memories, one sip at a time.

Drinklytics is your digital journal, allowing you to record, rate, and relive your tasting adventures. Never forget a remarkable drink again, build a rich, personal archive of every wine, beer, spirit or any other drink type you discover.

Track wine, beer, tea, any drinks

Whether you're a connoisseur of craft beers, a wine enthusiast, a spirits aficionado, or simply enjoy tea, soda, or any other beverage, Drinklytics has you covered. The app functions as your ultimate wine tasting journal, beer tasting companion, spirits record keeper or a generic drink tracker, making it easy to log notes for any drink you try.

Customize entries with tags & notes

Dive deep into your tasting experience. You can add comprehensive notes, personal scores, and flexible tags to every entry. Tailor your records with custom characteristics and tags, ensuring every detail of your unique tasting journey is captured exactly how you like it.

Refine your records, anytime.

Your private library of tasting adventures is always at your fingertips. Quickly access past entries to add new photos, edit tasting notes, or update any details you may have forgotten. With a tap, you can refine every record to ensure it's as accurate and complete as your experience.

Flexible search to rediscover every sip

Retrieves the perfect drink for the perfect moment. The simple but powerful search allows you to rediscover past entries using any custom tag you've added. Search by aroma, food pairing, occasion, or any other detail. Ensuring you can always pinpoint the right drink for the right occasion.

Customize your app in your language

The app adapts to you. You can choose from multiple languages to personalize your experience, ensuring every feature and detail feels right.

Don't see your language, or notice a translation that seems off? Simply use the app's "Send Feedback" section in the settings page to submit a request or report any issues. Your input helps me make the app better for everyone.

About

Hello, I'm Dario Tordoni, the developer of Drinklytics.

One day I bought a bottle of wine, and when I got home my wife reminded me that I had already tried that wine, and I didn’t even like it.

I've always had a terrible memory for these things. That's why I created Drinklytics.

I tried to make it flexible so that you can store multiple types of beverages.


I decided not to include social features. There's nothing wrong with wanting to share, but I believe that passions and hobbies can also exist in a private and intimate space. With Drinklytics, your experience is designed for you, free from the pressure of having to collect likes.


I'm really excited to share this first version with you! Think of it as the starting point: I'm passionately continuing to develop it, and I already have a lot of ideas for new functions that will make it richer and more powerful over time.


I’m a solo developer and work on this app in my spare time. While I do my best, it’s possible you might find a bug or two. If you do, please let me know! Your help is invaluable for making the app more stable and reliable. You can easily contact me using the feedback button in the app’s settings menu.

FAQ

What drinks can I record?

Any drink. From wine to beer, from water to tea. Soda, fruit juices, any type of drink.

What if I make a mistake recording a drink?

You can edit or delete it and create it again.

Is the app free?

Yes, the app is free and requires no subscriptions.

Are my data safe?

Absolutely. The app doesn’t ask for personal data, and the drinks you save stay on your device.

Why does the app request network access?

Because it can collect anonymous data about your usage to improve your experience, but only if you agree to do so.

Why does the app request media/camera access?

Because you can decide to add one or more photos to your drinks, and you can do so by taking a new photo or selecting one from your gallery. I don't use this permission for anything else, but I'm trying to figure out if it's possible not to use it, because I myself pay attention to these permissions when I install apps on my smartphone.

How do you make money?

I don’t. I developed this app because it addressed a personal need and because I wanted to learn how to build it. Since it might be useful to others, I want to share it with everyone.

How can I help you continue your project?

The best thing you can do is support the project by donating on my Ko-fi account, I will be grateful to you.

What if I don't want or can't donate money?

You can always share the app with your friends, write about it on your blog, or share it on social media. Help others find it, and you'll have helped me.

If I find an error or have an improvement idea?

I’m happy to receive your feedback, send me an email at tordoni.dario@gmail.com, thanks.
